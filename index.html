Goal

Be the first player to reach 100 score.

Gameplay

On a turn, a player rolls the dice repeatedly until either:

  • 1 is rolled
  • The player chooses to hold(stop rolling)

    • If the playe chooses to hold, all of the points rolled during that turn are added to his or her score.
